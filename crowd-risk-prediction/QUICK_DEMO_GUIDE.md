## 💡 Pro Recording Tips

1. **Use full screen** (F11) - cleaner look
2. **Hide bookmarks** (Ctrl+Shift+B)
3. **Zoom 100%** - best clarity
4. **Smooth mouse movements** - don't rush
5. **Pause between sections** - easier editing
6. **Speak clearly** - explain what you're showing
7. **Highlight with cursor** - draw attention
8. **Record in segments** - 1 min chunks easier to edit
