## 💡 Delivery Tips

### **Pace:**
- Speak at **130-150 words per minute**
- Pause for 2-3 seconds between sections
- Let viewers absorb visual information

### **Emphasis:**
- Stress key numbers: "**2-5 seconds**", "**97% accuracy**"
- Emphasize benefits: "**real-time**", "**automated**"
- Highlight uniqueness: "**predicts before incidents occur**"

### **Tone:**
- Professional but enthusiastic
- Confident in the technology
- Focus on safety impact
- End with strong call-to-action

### **Body Language (if on camera):**
- Smile naturally
- Use hand gestures to emphasize points
- Maintain eye contact with camera
- Show genuine excitement about the product
